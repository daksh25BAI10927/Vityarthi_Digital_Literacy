 
Social Media Do’s:
• Think before you post and consider long-term impact
• Maintain respectful and polite communication
• Protect your personal information and privacy
• Verify facts before sharing content
• Use professional language, especially on public profiles
Social Media Don’ts:
• Do not share offensive, abusive, or harmful content
• Avoid oversharing personal or sensitive information
• Do not engage in online arguments or trolling
• Avoid spreading misinformation or rumors
• Do not post inappropriate content that can harm your reputation
